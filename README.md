# 📊 Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the major challenges faced by telecom companies. This project analyzes customer behavior and predicts customer churn using Machine Learning techniques.

The objective is to identify factors responsible for customer attrition and provide business recommendations to improve customer retention.

---

* NumPy

* Seaborn
* Scikit-Learn

---

## 📂 Dataset

* Telco Customer Churn Dataset
* Total Records: 7043 customers
* Features: 21 columns

---

## 🎯 Model Performance

* Accuracy: Approximately 80%

---

## 💡 Business Insights

* Month-to-month customers have higher churn rates.
* Customers with high monthly charges churn more.
* Short-tenure customers are likely to leave.
* Long-term contracts reduce churn.
* Customers without tech support have higher churn.
